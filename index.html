Smart Product Pricing Engine

A multi-modal deep learning solution for the Amazon ML Challenge 2025 that predicts product prices from catalog text, product images, and numerical features using an ensemble of three transformer-based models (DistilBERT+ViT, RoBERTa+CLIP, DeBERTa+CLIP), evolved iteratively from an XGBoost baseline (55.60% SMAPE) to a final weighted ensemble achieving 43.5961% SMAPE on 75,000 products.
